Since its founding in 1991 as a collective of independent musicians, YAS! has come a long way & has been experienced as a meeting place for creating and sharing jazz.

Today, YAS! has become a music management company with a roster of over one hundred professional musicians trained through direct contact with the best of international jazz.

In various formats—big band, ensemble, Dixieland, marching band, orchestra, string groups, trios, quartets, and more—the collective of musicians offers a wide range of sounds and diverse performances, leading them to be considered by specialists and jazz enthusiasts alike as one of the best and most complete jazz groups on the current European scene.

But YAS!'s services also encompass the comprehensive organization of musical and educational events, handling both the purely organizational aspects and the technical and promotional ones.

Thanks to the effort and collaboration of its entire roster of artists, the collective also represents a cultural initiative that fosters greater access to music.

Through the creation of international seminars, courses, and workshops featuring leading figures in world jazz, this collective demonstrates its expertise and pedagogical skills. as evidenced by the success achieved, as well as the media and institutional recognition.
